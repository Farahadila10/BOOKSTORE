Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Pustaka Syafie Bookstore Inventory Management System?

The Pustaka Syafie Bookstore System is a web application that helps manage the stock of books and related materials available at the bookstore. It allows users to check book availability and enables admins to track, manage, and restock inventory efficiently.

2. Who can use the inventory management system?

The system is designed for both customers and administrators. Customers can view available books and check their availability, while administrators have access to inventory management tools, such as adding or updating stock levels and reviewing book usage.

3. Do I need an account to use the system?

Yes, both admins and users need an account to log in. Admins have access to inventory management tools, while users can browse the bookstore, check the availability of books, and place orders.

4. How can I check the availability of books in stock?

Customers can view available books directly from the bookstore’s main inventory page without logging in. This page shows the current stock levels of the books.

5. How do admins restock books?

Admins can access the admin dashboard after logging in. From there, they can view current stock levels, add new books, or update quantities to restock the books as needed.

6. Who can I contact if I have additional questions or need support?

If you have further questions, please reach out to the Pustaka Syafie support team or the designated administrator. They can help with any inquiries related to the bookstore and the system.
